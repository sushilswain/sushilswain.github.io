🔴 The Situation Before

Ajeets Manpower needed a Google Ads presence to generate qualified leads from businesses in Gulf nations (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain) and selected EU countries looking to hire manpower through their agency.

There was no structured campaign in place. The account needed to be built from the ground up with the right geographic segmentation, keyword strategy, and bidding approach. The challenge was targeting high-intent decision-makers across multiple international markets with very different search behaviors and competition levels.

Section 02

🛠️ What I Did — Strategy & Execution

The approach was to build a clean, geo-segmented campaign architecture that would allow precise budget control per region and clear performance visibility by market.

1
Region-Based Campaign Separation

Set up separate Google Search Ad campaigns for each major region — Gulf nations (UAE, Saudi, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain) and selected EU countries. This allowed individual budget control, bid adjustments, and performance tracking per market.

2
Keyword Clustering by Region & Service

Researched and grouped keywords based on both geographic intent and recruitment service type (skilled workers, blue-collar, white-collar). Created separate ad groups within each campaign for keyword-to-ad relevancy.

3
Search Term Optimisation

Continuously monitored search term reports after launch. Built and refined negative keyword lists to cut irrelevant traffic. Identified and promoted high-converting search terms as exact match keywords.

4
Bidding Strategy Progression

Launched with Maximise Clicks to gather 3 months of clean conversion data across all regions. After sufficient data, implemented Target CPA (tCPA) bidding — allowing Google's algorithm to optimise for leads at the lowest possible cost.

5
Landing Page Alignment

Worked with the client to ensure landing pages were aligned with each region's ad messaging and search intent. Suggested copy and CTA adjustments based on regional conversion data.
