Section 01

🔴 The Situation Before I Stepped In

When I took over the Aarav Infotech Google Ads account, it was generating just 3 leads per month at a cost per conversion of ₹824. The account was essentially on life support — burning budget with almost no measurable return.

The existing campaign was a single broad search campaign with no proper ad group structure, no audience segmentation, and no conversion tracking in place. Budget was being spent on irrelevant search terms, and there was no visibility into which regions, keywords, or landing pages were actually driving results.

Section 02

🛠️ What I Did — Strategy & Execution

I took a systematic, data-driven approach — starting with a full account audit and rebuilding the campaign architecture from scratch.

1
Region & Keyword Analysis

Identified highly-converting regions (tier-2 and select tier-1 cities) using GA4 and search term reports. Focused budget on top-performing locations and removed wasted spend on low-intent regions.

2
Keyword Research & Search Term Optimisation

Used Google Search Console, Keyword Planner, UberSuggest and Google Trends to identify high-CTR, high-intent keywords. Built a tight negative keyword list to eliminate irrelevant traffic.

3
Campaign Restructure — 4 Themed Ad Groups

Grouped keywords by relevancy into 4 distinct ad groups, each with its own dedicated landing page. Applied Dynamic Keyword Insertion (DKI) in ad copies to improve Quality Score and CTR.

4
Bidding Strategy Progression

Started with Maximise Clicks to gather data, then switched to Maximise Conversions, and finally implemented Target CPA (tCPA) bidding after 3 months of clean conversion data — significantly reducing CPA.

5
Ad Scheduling

Analysed conversion data by day and hour. Scheduled ads to run only during high-converting windows, reducing wasted impressions during off-peak hours.

6
Landing Page Optimisation via GA4

Analysed user journeys in GA4 — scroll depth, exit pages, CTA click-through. Recommended and implemented section changes and CTA repositioning on all 4 landing pages to improve conversion rates.

7
Conversion Tracking via GTM

Set up and tested all conversion actions (form submits, calls, email clicks) through Google Tag Manager. Created dedicated GA4 tags to send conversion events as custom events for deeper funnel analysis.

8
Custom Audiences & Remarketing

Identified customer signals and built custom audiences based on search keywords, past landing page visitors, and converters. Launched a Demand Gen remarketing campaign with multiple ad groups targeting each audience within a set frequency cap.
